Company Overview

Who We Are

MATCO is a professional electrical consulting and supplies company headquartered in Mokattam, Cairo. We serve clients across Egypt with a comprehensive portfolio of electrical products and solutions.

As the official Egyptian distributor for GEWISS — Italy's world-class electrical infrastructure manufacturer — we bring premium-grade products directly to our clients with full technical support and consulting.

Our expertise spans EV charging infrastructure, industrial electrical systems, residential accessories, and smart building solutions. We don't just supply products — we consult, design, and deliver complete systems.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide Egypt's residential, commercial, and industrial sectors with world-class electrical solutions — delivering reliability, safety, and innovation through trusted Italian-engineered products and expert local consulting.

Our Vision

To become Egypt's most trusted name in smart electrical infrastructure — leading the transition to sustainable EV charging networks and intelligent building systems, building a more connected and efficient future.

Official Partnership

MATCO × GEWISS

GEWISS is one of Europe's most respected electrical infrastructure manufacturers, headquartered in Bergamo, Italy. With over 50 years of innovation, GEWISS products are certified to the highest international standards.

As GEWISS's authorized Egyptian distributor, MATCO brings the full range of GEWISS products to the Egyptian market — EV charging stations, IP-rated enclosures, smart switches, and industrial isolators — with full local support.
